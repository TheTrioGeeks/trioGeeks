Our Story with Forest Exotic

Revolutionizing Exotic Pet Industry with 24/7 Animal Monitoring

The Story:

In the highly competitive reptiles and exotic pet industry, an entrepreneur decided to collaborate with us on a pioneering initiative: 24/7 animal monitoring live streams. These multiple livestreaming broadcasts enable customers worldwide to observe their prospective pets' behaviors firsthand, fostering trust and confidence before their purchases. Despite rumors circulating among competitors regarding animal health, our shared objective remains: to increase reliability, drive local and international sales, increase conversion rates, and improve animal care processes, thereby generating cost savings.

It totally exceeds my expectation. Not only in terms of my shop's reputation, my customers from all around the world decided to buy just purely by monitoring my reptiles though the system.

Well done Trio team!

Interactive Features to Drive Engagement

The platform provides exclusive livestream access to members, real-time pet habitat streaming, a pet routine schedule, a rewind feature for feeding times, multiple camera views, fullscreen video, and mobile optimization.

Member Only

Only allowed customers to access livestreams of pet they are interested in.

Live with the Pet

Display real-time videos stream of the pet's habitat or their enclosure.

Pet's Routine

Display schedule of Feeding time, Veggie time, and Lighting on period for customers to monitor their pet behavior.

Catch Up on Feeding Time

Allow customers to rewind the livestream to the pet's feeding time to see if they ate well for different time zone viewers.

Multiple Cameras, Multiple View

Display multiple cameras on the same page at the same time.

Go Fullscreen

Expand the video feed to full screen mode for an immersive viewing experience.

Mobile Ready

Ensure the front-end interface optimized for mobile.

Outcome and Achievements after Implementation

Sales Performance and Brand Reputation

  • Overseas customer conversion rate increased by 70%, leading to a remarkable 55% sales increase.
  • Local customer conversion rate surged by 40%, resulting in a 33% sales increase.
