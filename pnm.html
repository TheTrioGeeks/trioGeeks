Our Story with PET ‘N ME

In the dynamic landscape of Thailand's pet care market, a pioneering initiative has taken root. As a co-founder of the first omni-channel pet supply start-up under Central Group, we have overseen every aspect, from conducting exhaustive market research to passionately pitching our vision, crafting logo designs, designing the look and feel, mood, and tone of both offline and online stores, and more.

Our vision is clear: to introduce Thailand's premier omni-channel pet supply, exclusively developed under Central Group. By seamlessly integrating platforms such as ERP, E-POS, and e-commerce, our project stands as a beacon of affordability and operational efficiency.

With relentless dedication, we had successfully launched both online and offline stores, ensuring our systems were up and running seamlessly and on schedule. We recognize that the key to our success lies in delivering a cohesive, unparalleled customer experience.

Main Features We Provided

Backend

Offered a comprehensive backend solution, including inventory management across warehouses, order processing from various channels, a detailed product catalog system, and secure payment processing with multiple options

  • Stock, Supply Chain and Logistic Management:

    We've streamlined inventory management across multiple warehouses, allocated reserved stocks for each store, and implemented seamless delivery solutions by integrating with third-party logistics providers and self-collection points. These efforts have significantly improved operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

  • Order Management:

    We've also synchronized orders across online and offline channels, ensured real-time stock level deductions upon order placements, and managed the entire order journey from placement to fulfillment, covering processing, shipping, and returns for all channels. This has streamlined the client's operations and enhanced customer experience.

  • Product Catalog Management:

    We've organized a comprehensive category tree, compiled detailed item information including descriptions, photos, models, and variations, and maintained essential data fields like original prices, promotions, and costs. This meticulous approach has provided a structured and informative shopping experience for customers.

  • Payment:

    We've ensured secure and seamless payment processing, supported multiple payment methods including COD, digital wallets, and cards to cater to diverse preferences, and integrated fraud detection measures for enhanced security. These improvements have fortified the trust and convenience for our client's customers.
